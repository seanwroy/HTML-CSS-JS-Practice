Q: Who can take part in events?

A: This service is available for newcomers to Canada, including landed immigrants and refugees.


Q: Great! How do I sign up?

A: Click on the login/register links in the toolbar in order to create an account and a profile.


Q: How do events work?

A: Events are created by the recent immigrants or refugees for others in this community. Events can

consist of volunteer opportunities, sports/activity meet-ups, and social gatherings.


Q: What if I don't speak English?

A: That's okay! Hosts set the main spoken language (or it is set automatically) and attendees list their spoken languages on their profile.

Event pages are automatically translated into the preferred language in your profile for your convenience. This way, the majority of people attending events

Can speak the language they are the most comfortable speaking.
